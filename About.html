Learn About Us

Caring for seniors is a labor of love and requires a special person

United Care Homes is a leading domiciliary care, Live-in Care, and a Supported living company dedicated to providing high-quality care services to elderly and vulnerable people in the comfort of their own homes. With a team of experienced and compassionate caregivers, we strive to make a positive difference in the lives of our clients by offering personalized care that meets their unique needs and preferences.
Our company was founded with a vision to provide affordable and reliable home care services to those in need, and we have been fulfilling this mission since our establishment. We understand that every individual has unique care requirements, which is why we offer a range of services that can be customized to meet the specific needs of our clients. Our services include personal care, medication management, meal preparation, companionship, and more.
